# DMCA / Takedown Policy

**Operator:** Hemant
**Contact:** hemantkumar.bk@gmail.com (subject line: `DMCA`)

Audiobook-Assistant respects copyright. If you are a rights-holder and believe a user
has uploaded material that infringes your copyright, send a notice
including:

1. Your contact information (name, email, postal address).
2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed.
3. Identification of the allegedly infringing material — a URL to the
   document if accessible, or the document title + uploader email if not.
4. A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that you have a good-faith
   belief the use is unauthorised by the copyright owner, its agent, or
   the law.
5. A statement that the information in your notice is accurate.
6. Your physical or electronic signature.

We aim to acknowledge receipt within **72 hours** and act on valid
notices within **7 days** of acknowledgement.

## Counter-notice

If your content was removed and you believe the takedown was an error,
you may file a counter-notice with the same details + a statement that
you consent to jurisdiction of the courts of Bengaluru, India.

## Repeat infringers

Accounts subject to **3 valid takedowns** within a rolling 12-month
window are terminated. We retain the audit log of takedowns for 90 days.

## Indian context

DPDP Act / IT Act apply alongside DMCA-style takedowns. Indian
rights-holders may use the same channel; we accept notices in English,
Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Marathi, or Telugu.

---

Bad-faith takedowns may attract liability under §43A of the IT Act
and, in the US, §512(f) of the DMCA (knowing material misrepresentations).
